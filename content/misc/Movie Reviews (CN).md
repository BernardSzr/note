## Recent Reviews

### Dune: Part Two (2024)

> [!tip] Rating: ★★★★★
> Denis Villeneuve delivers a sci-fi masterpiece.

**Director**: Denis Villeneuve
**Genre**: Sci-Fi, Drama
**Runtime**: 166 minutes

> [!note] My Thoughts
> The visual storytelling is breathtaking. The sandworm riding scene alone is worth the price of admission.

**Pros:**
- Stunning cinematography
- Powerful performances
- Epic scale

**Cons:**
- Long runtime
- Complex plot for newcomers
