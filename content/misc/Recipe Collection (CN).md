## Quick Recipes

### 15-Minute Garlic Shrimp

> [!info] Servings: 2 | Time: 15 min | Difficulty: Easy

**Ingredients:**
- 300g shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1/4 cup white wine
- 2 tbsp fresh parsley
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Lemon wedges

**Instructions:**
1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium-high heat
2. Season shrimp with salt and pepper
3. Cook shrimp 2 minutes per side until pink
4. Add garlic, cook 30 seconds until fragrant
5. Deglaze with white wine
6. Garnish with parsley and lemon

> [!tip] Pro Tip
> Don't overcook the shrimp! They're done as soon as they turn pink and curl.

### One-Pot Pasta Primavera

> [!info] Servings: 4 | Time: 25 min | Difficulty: Easy

```markdown
## Ingredients
- 400g spaghetti
- 2 cups mixed vegetables
- 3 cloves garlic, sliced
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 cup vegetable broth
- 1/4 cup fresh basil
- Parmesan cheese

## Instructions
1. Cook pasta al dente
2. Sauté vegetables until tender-crisp
3. Add garlic, cook 1 minute
4. Toss pasta with vegetables
5. Add broth, season, and serve
```
