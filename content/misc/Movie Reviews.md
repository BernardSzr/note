## Recent Reviews

### Dune: Part Two (2024)

> [!tip] Rating: ★★★★★
> Denis Villeneuve delivers a sci-fi masterpiece that expands on the first film in every way.

**Director**: Denis Villeneuve
**Genre**: Sci-Fi, Drama
**Runtime**: 166 minutes

> [!note] My Thoughts
> The visual storytelling is breathtaking. The sandworm riding scene alone is worth the price of admission. The political intrigue deepens, and the performances are stellar.

**Pros:**
- Stunning cinematography
- Powerful performances
- Faithful to the source material
- Epic scale

**Cons:**
- Long runtime may deter some viewers
- Complex plot for newcomers

> [!info] Connection
> The themes of power and destiny relate to [[Book Notes - Thinking Fast and Slow|decision-making under uncertainty]].

### The Zone of Interest (2023)

> [!tip] Rating: ★★★★
> A haunting, unconventional film about the banality of evil.

**Director**: Jonathan Glazer
**Genre**: Drama, History
**Runtime**: 105 minutes

> [!warning] Content Warning
> This film depicts the Holocaust through a unique lens. It's disturbing but important.

> [!note] My Thoughts
> The sound design is extraordinary — what you don't see is more powerful than what you do. It's a masterclass in subtlety and restraint.

### Past Lives (2023)

> [!tip] Rating: ★★★★★

**Director**: Celine Song
